blood donation

It is a voluntary medical procedure in which blood or one of its components is transferred from a healthy person to a sick person in need of blood. This procedure is needed by millions of people every year; It is used during surgery, accidents, or some diseases that require the transfusion of some blood components.

The amount of blood donated

An adult in good health can donate approximately (450-500 ml) of his blood without any fears or risks to his health. He can donate every two months, so that the number of times he donates does not exceed 5 times a year.
